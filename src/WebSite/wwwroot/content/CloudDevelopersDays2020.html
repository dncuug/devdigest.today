The third edition of the conference Cloud DeveloperDays will take place on May 19-20 at the EXPO Krakow International Exhibition and Congress Center. The event is addressed to programmers and IT architects creating software implemented or integrated with cloud platforms such as Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud or Amazon Web Services.

Cloud DeveloperDays is a unique event in the IT sector. Dozens of participants from around the world will meet to listen to the best speakers from the industry during two intensive days. In the course of the conference many issues related to platforms, technologies, tools and practical problems encountered by people working in the cloud will be raised.

- Based on our experience in organizing conferences, we know how important the right choice of speakers is. We pay special attention to ensure that each of them is an authority in their field and provides as much knowledge as possible to other participants. During CDD our guests will be, among others Glenn Colpaert, Azure MVP, Chris Van Sluijsveld, Cloud Scientist, Geert Van Der Cruijsen Cloud Native Architect, Jiří Činčura Microsoft MVP and many others. There will also be Polish specialists – ensure the organizers.

- Cloud DeveloperDays are two very intense days, during which every participant will have the opportunity to find something for himself. After the theoretical part, we have planned, as every year, an After Party during which it will be possible to ask about specific issues, make contacts and exchange experience in a favorable atmosphere - they emphasize.

The event is organized by Data Master, the organizer of many other IT conferences in recent years, such as NET DeveloperDays or JOIN! The Database Conference. As the previous events, Cloud DeveloperDays is going to present high professional level. Some speakers and the introductory agenda are already revealed.

Participation in the conference is paid, and tickets are available on the conference website. The official language of the event is English.

More information, the opportunity to submit a presentation and purchase tickets are available via the event website.
